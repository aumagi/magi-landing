Bearing gifts of comfort and joy

An annual project of the South Carolina School of the Arts Dept. of Art+Design

Every year, Magi provides clothing and Santa gifts for children who might otherwise wake to nothing on Christmas morning. The number of children served by Magi has grown steadily, but is currently being held at 100 because of financial and logistical factors. But why stop at 100 when we could help so many more?
